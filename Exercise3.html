How to Cook Rice

Material Needed

  • 1kg Rice
  • Pot
  • 2 cups of water

Instruction

  • Rinse the water 3times
  • Prepare a pot with water
  • Boil the water
  • After Boiling put the rice
  • Wait 40 minutes to cook
  • Off the gas and open the Pot and simmer the rice
